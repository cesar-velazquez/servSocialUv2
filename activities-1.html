Section 1. Activities

Activity 01. Action plan  

Instructions

  1. Once you have carried out your needs analysis and analysed the strengths and needs of your receiving institution, you should develop an action plan to address one of the needs identified. Read the Action plan booklet before you begin, and use the Action plan template to develop your own plan. I strongly recommend that you work with the person supervising your placement at the institution when making your plan.
  2. Upload your Action plan in the corresponding activity in Eminus. Use the template provided as follows (Action plan).

Activity 02. Project outline  

Instructions

  1. Once you have developed your Action plan, it is necessary that you write a Project outline, detailing your objectives and activities at the receiving institution. Consult the format attached to this activity, and write your own outline following the instructions provided.
  2. Please note that the outline should be written in Spanish, as it is an official requirement of the University for the registration of your SS.
  3. Upload your Project Outline in the corresponding activity in Eminus. You can find as follows the template, and three examples (where all the personal and official information has been deleted). You’ll notice that the examples are very varied, and your project outlines may vary to. The examples are only for you to have a clearer idea of what a project outline may look like.
