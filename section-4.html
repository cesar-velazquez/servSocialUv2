Section 4. Monthly reports and completing your Social Service

Monthly reports

You have to upload your monthly reports in the corresponding activities in Eminus every month. Remember you can find the form for these reports in Eminus, in Important Documents. The signatures and seal on them must be in BLUE INK, and must NOT appear on a separate blank page at the end. If your report takes more than one page, make sure to include part of its text on the last page along with the signatures and seal.

If you are teaching, remember that for every ‘teaching hour’ you have to report a ‘planning hour’. Therefore, if you teach, for example, 5 hours a week, you should also include in the same report 5 planning hours for them. For your first report, you should also consider witnin the ‘planning hours’ the time you have dedicated to the needs analysis and the development of your action plan and project outline.

You must also consider that 80 of the 480 hours of your Social Service correspond to the development of your e-portfolio, which must be included along the process.

Final e-portfolio

Once you have made sure you have included in your e-portfolio all the required content and any other content you have decided to share, post your blog URL in the corresponding activity in Eminus (Final e-portfolio).

This e-portafolio is 4.0 points from your total grade.

Completing your Social Service

Once you have completed your Social Service (480 hours), download the following file to see an example of the Carta de liberación  that the corresponding authorities of your Social Service placement have to provide you with to state that you have completed it. This letter must also be on a headed paper from the institution, and signed and sealed IN BLUE. Once it is ready, upload it in the corresponding activity in Eminus.

Enlace Carta de liberación Word document

The punctual submission of your monthly reports is 2.0 points from your total grade.

These points will be added once you upland your Carta de liberación  in the activity Completing your Social Service in Eminus.
